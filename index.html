Robots with parachutes

Two robots are to be parachuted onto random locations on an infinite line. When they land, their parachutes detach and remain where they are. The robots may be programmed from the following instruction set:

  • Go left one unit (left)
  • Go right one unit (right)
  • Skip next instruction if there is a parachute here (skip)
  • Go to label (goto label)
Each of these instruction can include a label (label: instruction).
Program the robots to collide.
